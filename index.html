About Me

Born and raised in Haiti, I am personally passionnate about Social Entepreneurship and the ability of technology to impact change in developing countries. I am a recent graduate from Rutgers University with a Bachelor in Information Technology and a minor in Economics, an education that was made possible thanks to the presistiougious Jack Kent Cooke scholarship. I am a challenge seeking person, constantly looking for ways to improve myself and for things to build. I take joy in planting seeds and seeing things grow, which is where I find my work ethic and motiviation to work on new projects. This is why, I might just be the right addition to your team :)
